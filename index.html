Chips & Dairy

Rebranding

This was a re-branding assignment of a local business in Ottawa, Ontario. I chose Chips and Dairy. A Canadian and Chinese resturant, the challenge I was faced with was how to show the identity of the resturant. Therefore I went with a very clean western type face, while the ampersand sign mimics a soup bowl with chopsticks. Thus tying in the asain culture.
